What is it?

The purpose of this app is to help visualize and compare crypotcurrency price data from different exchanges

This is still a work in progress in its early stages

You can hover over most of the buttons to learn their functionality with their help bubbles. After you are comfortable with all the buttons, you can turn the help bubbles off in the settings menu all the way to the right.

Initial Configuration

Use the Gear icon top right to change app behavior like turning off the help tooltips and making charts undraggable. You can also use the settings to set which charts you want to display by default. Once you close the settings window, your settings are saved and reloaded when you refresh the browser. Close the browser to reset your preferences.

All individual components such as charts, sontrols and other data components can be dragged and repositioned anywhere on the page. You can turn this functionality on or off in the settings menu. The draggability interferes with certain actions sometimes

Charts can also be resized

Functionality

You have the main price chart front and center. You can switch between different coins and exchanges as well as time units and periods to your hearts content

You can use the controls in the top tight to manipulate the different data sets displayed on the chart

Add multiple price lines to the main price chart to compare them.

When adding multiple prices, it is important to note that most of the price indicators will still only reflect your first data set. The Spread indicator will show the spread beween the first and second line and needs to have at least two price lines on the chart to be active.

You can easily overlay and compare two prices on the same graph. Change their color so you know which one is which.

When you want to view two or more coin prices whose prices are too far apart, like Ripple and Bitcoin for example, you can scale them in relation to one another and as a % of their initial value using the controls on the right.

Use the magenta line ruler on the right to help you analyze prices.

The Algo Tester tool that you bring up with the biohazard button is still in very early experimental stages.
